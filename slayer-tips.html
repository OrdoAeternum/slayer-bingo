OSRS Slayer 81 — One‑Page Cheatsheet + Block/Prefer Lists

Fast XP • High Points • Profitable Loot

Updated: Aug 31, 2025

Fast XP Fast XP (Cannon Tasks)

Prioritize dense, cannonable tasks for the best XP/hr at level 81. Keep 1–2k cballs and Stamina pots ready.

1) Dagannoths (Lighthouse)
  • • Method: Place cannon centrally; tag spawns with Blowpipe or RCB + Broad Bolts.
  • • Gear: Elite Void or max Ranged; Ava’s; Blowpipe/RCB; cannon.
  • • Inventory: 1–2k cballs, 2–4 Prayer pots, Stamina, teleports, some food.
  • • Notes: Multi-area, fast respawns; pray Melee if face-tanking, step back to limit damage.
2) Kalphites (Kalphite Lair – Workers/Soldiers)
  • • Method: Cannon in the open Worker room; safe-spot around corners if needed.
  • • Gear: Ranged with Proselyte/Elite Void; Antipoison/Antidote; cannon.
  • • Inventory: 1–2k cballs, Antipoison, Prayer pots, Stamina, food.
  • • Notes: Very consistent XP; minimal travel once fairy ring configured (BIQ).
3) Suqahs (Lunar Isle)
  • • Method: Cannon in the north fields; keep Protect from Melee on.
  • • Gear: Ranged (Elite Void/Proselyte) + cannon; Lunar staff/teleport access.
  • • Inventory: 1–2k cballs, Prayer pots, Stamina, teleports, food.
  • • Notes: High density and easy pathing; bring seal of passage if needed.

Points Points Strategy (Turael Boost)

  • • Goal: Farm large streak bonus points while keeping tasks fast.
  • • Loop: Do tasks 1–9 at Turael (very quick), then take the 10th at Konar or Nieve/Steve for the big streak bonus.
  • • Streak: Aim for 100+ to maximize point payouts on milestone tasks.
  • • Skipping: If you get a slow task for your milestone, visit Turael to reassign without breaking your streak.
  • • Extras: Use Bracelet of Slaughter to extend favorite tasks; Expeditious bracelet to speed through ones you’re finishing before a milestone.

Loot Profitable Loot Tasks

Do these when you want steady GP with solid XP.

Gargoyles (75 Slayer)
  • • GP/hr: ~600k steady.
  • • Method: Melee on Slayer Tower rooftop or tower floors; unlock “Gargoyle Smasher.”
  • • Gear: Melee DPS (whip or better), Prayer gear if flicking; bring Rock hammer.
  • • Tips: High-alch large drops; bring Nature runes/Law runes for tele.
Nechryael (80 Slayer)
  • • GP/hr: ~0.8–1.2M when bursting/barraging in Catacombs.
  • • Method: Ancient Magicks; group them and burst; use altar to sustain Prayer.
  • • Gear: Prayer bonus + Magic gear; optional cannon for melee method.
  • • Tips: Bracelet of Slaughter to extend; bank noted seeds/runes.
Kurasks (70 Slayer)
  • • GP/hr: ~400–500k consistent.
  • • Method: Must use leaf-bladed weapons or broad ammo; Protect from Melee.
  • • Gear: Leaf-bladed battleaxe/sword (melee) or Rune c’bow + Broad Bolts (ranged).
  • • Tips: Alch herbs/armor; bring anti-poison if using the Fremennik Dungeon.

Boss Vorkath (Ranged) — Ready Reference

  • • Weapon: Dragon Hunter Crossbow. Start with Ruby (e) bolts, swap to Diamond (e) bolts sub-35–40% HP.
  • • Armor: Elite Void Ranger helm/top/robes/gloves for +12.5% dmg and +10% accuracy set bonus.
  • • Offhand: Dragonfire Ward (or Anti-dragon shield variants).
  • • Jewelry: Necklace of Anguish, Archer’s (i) ring, Ava’s Assembler, Pegasian boots.
  • • Potions: Ranging, Super/Extended Antifire, Anti-venom+, Stamina, Restores.
  • • Prayers: Protect from Magic; Rigour if unlocked.
  • • Notes: Walk during acid phase; dodge specials; keep antifire active at all times.

Blocks Block List (Always Block at 81)

Task Why Block Notes
Steel/Bronze/Iron Dragons Very slow kills, can’t cannon; high supply drain Save for when you have dragon-hunter setups.
Spiritual Creatures Spread out; need god items/KC; poor XP/GP Time-consuming at 81.
Wyrms Slower kills, attention-heavy, large task sizes Do later with stronger gear/levels.
Trolls / Ice Trolls Travel time + mediocre XP Only do if you need Frem boots unlocks.
Hellhounds No loot; Cerb not available until 91 Slayer Revisit at 91 for Cerberus.

Optional Optional Prefers (Unlocks)

  • • Kurasks — Consistent cash; leaf-bladed setups.

Flexible Skip-But-Don’t-Block

  • • Drakes — Decent uniques but slow; do when you want variety.
  • • Black Demons — Good with cannon in Chasm or if you do Demonic Gorillas; otherwise slow.

Checklist Quick Inventory Lists

  • • Bursting Pack: Runes (Blood/Ice), Prayer pots, Food, Rune pouch, Ancient spellbook, Salve (if applicable), Slaughter bracelets.

Tips Task/Point Tips

  • • Extend favorites (Nechs, Gargoyles, Dust Devils, Bloodvelds, Greater Demons).
  • • Keep a 100+ streak for maximum 10th/50th/100th task point bonuses.
  • • Prefer weights stack; don’t over-dilute—pick 3–5 favorites.
  • • Konar on milestones for big points + Brimstone keys.
